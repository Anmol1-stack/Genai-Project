### SOP Title
Water Puddle on Hospital Floor Standard Operating Procedure

### Scope
This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) applies to all hospital staff, visitors, and patients who encounter a water puddle on the hospital floor. The purpose of this SOP is to ensure prompt and effective response to water puddles to prevent slipping hazards, maintain a clean environment, and minimize the risk of accidents.

### Trigger Indicators
The following indicators trigger this SOP:
- Visible puddle on floor
- Wet floor warning sign absent
- Water stains on ceiling or walls
- Dripping from above
- Reflective wet surface
- Reports of water spillage or leakage

### Severity Policy
All water puddles on the hospital floor are considered medium-severity incidents, requiring immediate attention to prevent accidents and ensure patient safety.

### Department Routing
All reports of water puddles on the hospital floor will be routed to the Facilities Management department for resolution.

### First Response Actions
Upon notification of a water puddle, the following first response actions will be taken:
1. **Place wet floor warning signs** immediately around the affected area to alert staff, visitors, and patients of the slipping hazard.
2. **Mop and dry the area** as soon as possible to prevent further accidents.
3. **Identify and fix the source of the water** to prevent recurrence.
4. **Check for electrical hazards** nearby to ensure the safety of staff, visitors, and patients.

### Escalation Chain
If the initial response is insufficient or the issue is not resolved within the specified SLA, the following escalation chain will be activated:
1. Housekeeping Staff
2. Facilities Supervisor
3. Safety Officer
4. Hospital Administrator

### SLA Targets
The target resolution time for water puddles on the hospital floor is **1 hour** from the time of reporting.

### Human Review Triggers
The following triggers require human review:
- Reports of physical harm caused by a water puddle
- Recurring incidents of water puddles in the same area
- Inadequate or delayed response to a water puddle report

### Audit Fields
The following fields will be audited to ensure compliance with this SOP:
- Time of report
- Location of water puddle
- Response time
- Resolution time
- Root cause of the water puddle
- Effectiveness of the response and resolution actions
